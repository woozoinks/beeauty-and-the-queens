mission statement !

our mission as beeauty and the queens is to spread positivity,
inform others on mental health,
and provide resources for those struggling.
we want to bring awareness to the mental health community by
providing useful tools for everyone.
as a group that holds this topic close to our hearts,
it is very important for us to share these things with the world.
we are aware of the hardships that some people go through and
hope that we can provide some assistance through our efforts.

meaning behind beeauty and the queens

bees symbolize community and personal power.
we want to show the idea that in this community,
we are all queens and have the power to better ourselves.
we can all take control of ourselves
and lead the life we want to live in our given circumstances.
we have the power to see the beauty.
we are all queen bees.

mental illness

mental illness (sometimes called mental disorder) is
a wide range of conditions that affect mood, thinking and/or behavior.
mental illness is often caused by environmental stresses,
genetic factors, chemical imbalance, or a combination of these.
through medications or lifestyle changes, many people who
suffer with a mental illness learn to cope with it in their life.
